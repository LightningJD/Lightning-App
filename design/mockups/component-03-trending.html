What this is

A ranked list of the most popular testimony hashtags/topics trending across the platform. Each row shows rank number, hashtag, and testimony count for the week.

What you have now

Nothing. No hashtag system, no trending topics, no way to see what themes are popular. Testimonies don't have tags.

What it requires

Hashtag/tag system on testimonies (either user-added or AI-generated from testimony text), aggregation logic to count and rank by time period, and enough testimony volume to make "trending" meaningful.

Phase 2 üåê
Trending needs global volume to work
"Trending" only makes sense when you have enough testimony volume for topics to actually trend. With one church, you might get 5-10 testimonies a week ‚Äî that's not enough data for a ranked trending list to feel alive or change meaningfully.

What it needs to work: Hundreds of testimonies per week across multiple churches, a hashtag/tag system on testimonies (either manual or AI-extracted), and aggregation logic to rank by time window.

Why Phase 2: This is a discovery feature ‚Äî it helps people find testimonies across the global platform. In MVP (single church, testimony directory), users already know each other. They don't need an algorithm to surface what's popular. When Lightning opens to the world with self-discovery mode, that's when trending becomes essential.
