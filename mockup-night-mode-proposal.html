Key Differences: Current vs Proposed

  • Base background: #0a0a0a (pure black) → #0e1525 (deep navy) — warmer, less harsh

  • Accent colors: Blue (#4F96FF) stays primary; gold for testimonies; green for online status
  • Overall feel: Less OLED-dark, more Discord/Slack sophisticated dark theme
